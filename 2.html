Finance Analytics

Move from Excel to automated financial analytics.

Schedule a Data Strategy Meeting

Finance and accounting departments are constantly under pressure to deliver accurate, time sensitive analysis and advice to the business. But in an era of digital transformation where organizational data tools continue to proliferate, the data needed for analysis often lives in a growing number of sources.

We help financial professionals move away from manual data prep and report generation in Excel to automated financial analytics solutions that empower users to perform more meaningful analysis and add value to the bottom line.

Data and Analytics Services for Internal Finance Departments

Whether you operate a single entity or dozens of divisions or subsidiaries, we deliver financial data and analytics solutions that harness data from all of your sources—NetSuite, SAP ERP, Oracle EBS, JD Edwards, Sage, Lawson, Microsoft Dynamics, Infor, Everest, and more. With strong financial analytics, your business can make the best possible financial decisions more quickly, reduce costs, manage risks, and improve profitability and transparency.

Financial Planning and Analysis

Automate regular reporting and analysis of forecasting, budgets, financial plans, and cash flow.

Revenue and Profitability Management

Analyze cost and revenue drivers and identify ways to maximize profit, reduce operational costs, segment customers, and enhance profitable services/products.

Expense Management

Analyze large volumes of data from multiple locations and business lines
to get full visibility across costs and operating expenses. Uncover ways to save money, and improve expense management processes.
